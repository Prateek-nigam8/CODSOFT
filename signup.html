User Instructions

• To create a new account, enter your email address, username, and a strong password in the appropriate fields.

• Make sure to enter a valid email address that you have access to, as this will be used to verify your account and recover your password if needed.

• Retype your password in the "Confirm Password" field to make sure you've entered it correctly.

• If you want to see the password you are typing, click the "Show Password" checkbox. This will display the password in plain text.

• After completing the form, click the "Signup" button to submit your information and create your account.

• If you already have an account, click the "Login" link to go to the login page and enter your credentials to access your account.
