User Instruction

• Enter your username: Type in your username into the "Username" field. Your username should be provided by the website or application that you are trying to login to.

• Enter your password: Type in your password into the "Password" field. Your password should be kept confidential and known only to you.

• Show Password (Optional): If you would like to view the characters you are typing in the password field, you can check the "Show Password" box. This is helpful if you need to verify that you are typing the correct password.

• Click on the "Login" button: Once you have entered your username and password, click on the "Login" button to submit your login credentials and gain access to the website or application.

• Sign up for an account (Optional): If you do not have an account, click on the "Signup" link located below the login form to be redirected to the sign up page.
